List Reviews

Production
https://api.salla.dev/admin/v2
Production
https://api.salla.dev/admin/v2
GET
https://api.salla.dev/admin/v2
/reviews
Last modified:2025-05-07 09:20:04
This endpoint allows you to list product review, general product, and shipping ratings as well as Merchant store reviews.
Review Types
Product reviews are of type rating.
General product questions are of type ask.
Ratings about shipping are of type shipping.
Merchant store reviews are of type testimonial.
Scopes
reviews.read- Reviews Read Only

Request

Authorization
Add the parameter
Authorization
to Headerswhose value is to concatenate the Token after the Bearer.
Example:
Authorization: Bearer ********************
